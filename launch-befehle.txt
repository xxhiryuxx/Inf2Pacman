MacOS: (Homebrew)

Compiling-Command:
g++ -std=c++17 main.cpp -o pacman \
-I/opt/homebrew/opt/raylib/include \
-L/opt/homebrew/opt/raylib/lib \
-lraylib \
-framework OpenGL -framework Cocoa -framework IOKit -framework CoreVideo

Start über Terminal: ./pacman


Windows:
